kiks in-company training & consultancy solutions

The effectiveness of our in-company training and consultancies depends primarily on the understanding of your "real" needs (not your perceived needs) and applying the most appropriate trainer and training & consultancy solutions. Again, depending on the nature of your needs and how tailored or custom-designed you would like your training to be, we can offer you a number of in-company solutions, such as:

  • Delivering existing kiks in-company courses refined through pre-course discussions between you (partner-client) and us (provider).
  • Taking existing kiks public courses and tailor the content and delivery through Training Needs Analysis (TNA) with our trainer and your key people.
  • Designing a completely tailored training course, programme or consultancy solution for you including such elements as person-to-person coaching & mentoring, competency profiling and assessing.

Upon contacting our representative, you will be briefed with our in-company training process, which starts with us gaining an initial understanding of your training needs whereby initial solutions may be proposed and prices may be quoted by us. To discuss your requirements in further details, please call a member of our team on 0063-9195311875 or email to fdc@kiks.ph

Benefits of kiks in-company training & consultancy solutions

  • Delivered on-site or off-site, enabling you to utilize your internal facilities or to arrange external facilities of your choice.
  • Flexible timing and locations to suit the needs of your organization.
  • Tailored to meet your specific training & consultancy needs.
  • Seasoned practitioner-trainers and consultants with modern and down-to-earth training styles.
